Welcome!

Whether we're seeing you in Seattle or you're meeting us in St. Louis, here's your portal to all things Harper-Horner Wedding.

Kris

Kris ("Alaska") Harper grew up in Fairbanks, Alaska. In 2007, he came to Chicago with an enormous backpack and a thirst for knowledge. Now he lives in New York City, working as a programmer in the world of finance.

Paula

Paula Horner is at school in New York, after being at school in Seattle, after being at school in Chicago, after being at school in St. Louis. After graduation, she will probably switch things up by going to school, location to-be-determined.

Us

She was a bubbly orientation aide. He was an eager young first year. UChicago's Wick House brought them together; geography and circumstance kept them apart - for a while. Now, after having spent quite enough time in this

long-distance relationship business, they've finally made their way to the same city. With a cozy apartment, two crazy kittens, and plenty of adventuring to do, the most exciting question is now: What's next?
